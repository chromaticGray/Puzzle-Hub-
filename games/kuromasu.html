Rules (Range / Kuromasu)

  • Color some squares black, leaving others white.
  • Numbered squares must stay white and cannot be colored black.
  • No two black squares can be adjacent horizontally or vertically (diagonals OK).
  • All white squares must be connected - you can reach any white square from any other using only white squares.
  • Each number indicates the total count of white squares visible from that square in all four directions (up, down, left, right) until hitting a black square or wall. The numbered square itself is included in the count.

Controls: Tap a white cell to make it black. Tap a black cell to mark it with a dot (maybe white). Tap a dot to return it to white.
